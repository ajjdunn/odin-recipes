Oatmeal

Sometimes all you want is a steaming hot bowl of oatmeal before a day in the salt mines. Cheap, easy, and leaves you full until lunch time!

Ingredients

  • Steel Cut Oats
  • Brown Sugar
  • Hemp Hearts
  • Fruit you like

Steps

  1. Bring 2 cups of water to a boil in a medium saucepan
  2. Add 1 cup Steel Cut Oats
  3. Cook for 5 minutes or until desired consistancy
  4. Add all the other stuff to taste
  5. Enjoy that bastard! Careful not to burn your mouth!

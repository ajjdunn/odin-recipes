Vegan Lasagna

It's good to know that while you mindlessly devour this Lasagna in front of the TV over the next 8 minutes, no sentient creatures needed to be murdered.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Vegan cheese
  • Eggplant
  • Spinach
  • Vegan ground round

Steps

  1. Add layer of noodles
  2. Add layer of cheese
  3. Add layer on eggplant
  4. Add layer of spinach
  5. Add layer of ground round
  6. Repeat above steps until desired lasagna height reached
